NBA Career:

Kobe was drafted in 1996 by the Charlotte Hornets but traded to the Lakers. In his first all-star first appearance he was in the Slam dunk contest and won it. Kobe is a 18 time all-star and a member of the all NBA team 15 times. Also, a 12 time all- defensive team. He lead the NBA in scoring for two seasons and rank third on both season and playoff scoring list. Has won five NBA championships all with the lakers.
